HILFIX OFFERS THE HIGHEST QUALITY AND SERVICE FOR TELEPHONE AND COMPUTER / MAC REPAIR IN HILVERSUM

WELCOME TO HILFIX REPAIRENTER

You have a problem with a phone, tablet or computer. HilFix Repaircenter is THE party for a good and honest repair. Your phone or computer/mac is in good hands with us. Whether it concerns a broken screen or new battery in your iPhone/Samsung or you are looking for a refurbished iPhone or iPad, HilFix Repaircenter takes care of this with the utmost care and accuracy. We will handle your (privacy sensitive) data securely. After diagnosis, you will always receive a quotation and we will only proceed after approval. You are very welcome!

SPEED AND SERVICE

Fast service is important to us. We understand that you don't want to be without your device for too long and weusually carry out a telephone repair within half an hour . Unfortunately, this is not possible with motherboard repairs. Then the lead time is between 4 and 10 working days.
